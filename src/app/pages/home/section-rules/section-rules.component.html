Rules


  1. Do not destroy or take anything during exploration
  2. Behave calmly, be quiet
  3. Do not expose places that are secret
  4. Do not leave anything behind you except your footprints
  5. Photography is allowed only without the flash because of risk of exposing the group

Instructions


  1. Be everywhere in time, who is late is not coming
  2. Don't bring your friend with you, only the ones with permission. If you do, you and your friend will be excluded from the trip
  3. Follow the guide exact orders, if not you will be excluded
  4. Do not share any information about meeting place, time and location with anyone, if you do, you won't be able to come next time
